Frequently Asked Questions

Have Questions?

Find answers to the most common questions about TechMart

🛒 How do I place an order?

Simply browse our products, click "Add to Cart" on items you want to purchase, then proceed to checkout. You'll need to create an account or log in, provide your shipping address, and confirm your order.

💳 What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods including M-Pesa, credit/debit cards, and bank transfers. All payments are processed securely to protect your financial information.

🚚 How long does delivery take?

Delivery times vary depending on your location:

  • Nairobi & surrounding areas: 1-2 business days
  • Major cities (Mombasa, Kisumu, Nakuru, etc.): 2-4 business days
  • Other locations: 3-7 business days

📦 How can I track my order?

Once you place an order, you can track its status by logging into your account and visiting the "Orders" page. You'll see the current status of all your orders (Pending, Processing, Shipped, or Delivered).

🔄 What is your return policy?

We offer a 7-day return policy for unopened and unused items in their original packaging. If you receive a defective or damaged product, please contact us within 48 hours of delivery. Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after we receive the returned item.

✅ Are your products authentic?

Yes! All products sold at TechMart are 100% authentic and sourced from authorized distributors. We guarantee the quality and authenticity of every item in our store.

🛡️ Do products come with warranty?

Most electronic products come with a manufacturer's warranty. The warranty period varies by product and manufacturer. Check the product description for specific warranty information, or contact our customer support team for details.

💰 Can I cancel or modify my order?

You can cancel or modify your order if it's still in "Pending" status. Once an order moves to "Processing" or "Shipped" status, cancellation may not be possible. Contact our customer support team immediately if you need to make changes to your order.

📍 Do you offer pickup services?

Yes! You can pick up your order from our store at Joskim Annex, Jerusalem Avenue, Juja. Select "Store Pickup" during checkout, and we'll notify you when your order is ready for collection.

🎁 Do you offer bulk discounts?

Yes, we offer special pricing for bulk orders. If you're interested in purchasing multiple units of the same product or placing a large order, please contact our sales team at support@techmart.com for a custom quote.
